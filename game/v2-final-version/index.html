How to Play Pig Royale

  • Click "Hit" to draw two cards
  • Card values are added to your score
  • If you draw an Ace, your turn ends
  • If you draw two Aces, your score resets to 0
  • Click "Stand" to end your turn
  • First player to reach 100 points wins!
